Today, I design **AI-powered systems, automation pipelines, and data-driven applications** that are shaped around real human behavior—not just technical requirements.

I don’t just build features.

Most AI and software systems fail at one thing:

I focus on bridging that gap by building systems that:

- translate user behavior into system intelligence
- reduce friction through automation
- turn data into decisions, predictions, and actions
- align product logic with real human intent
